LEGAL INTELLIGENCE BRIEF: COURT HIERARCHY & JURISDICTION (2026)
1. THE SUPERIOR COURTS

The superior courts are established under Article 162 of the Constitution of Kenya:

  • Supreme Court: Apex court; exclusive jurisdiction over Presidential election disputes and appeals on matters of general public importance.
  • Court of Appeal: Appellate jurisdiction only; reviews decisions of the High Court and specialized courts.
  • High Court: Unlimited original jurisdiction in all civil and criminal matters.
  • Environment & Land Court (ELC): Equal status to High Court; exclusive jurisdiction over land and environmental disputes.
