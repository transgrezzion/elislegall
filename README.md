# Eli’s Legal Enterprise 🇰🇪
### Premier Legal Intelligence & Research Hub

Welcome to the official repository for **Eli’s Legal Enterprise**. This platform is dedicated to analyzing the Kenyan legal atmosphere, providing "Smart Briefs" on core legislation, and offering resources for civil litigation and succession matters.

## ⚖️ Core Focus Areas
* **Civil Procedure:** In-depth analysis of the Civil Procedure Act (Cap 21) and the "Overriding Objective."
* **Land Law:** Intelligence entries on the Land Registration Act (2012), focusing on Section 79 (Rectification).
* **Succession Law:** Practical guides for Intestacy and obtaining Grants of Representation.
* **Jurisdiction Guides:** Updated pecuniary limits for Kenyan Magistrates and Superior Courts.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
* **Hosting:** GitHub Pages
* **Format:** Static HTML5 / CSS3
* **Compliance:** Integrated Data Privacy notices following the Kenya Data Protection Act (2019).

## 📂 Repository Structure
* `index.html`: The main landing page and Client Portal.
* `kenya-court-jurisdiction.html`: A printable legal brief on the court hierarchy.
* `assets/`: (Planned) Storage for legal templates and PDF resources.

## ⚠️ Legal Disclaimer
The contents of this repository are for **informational and research purposes only**. Accessing this data or using the provided templates does not establish an advocate-client relationship. Please refer to our full disclaimer on the live site regarding "Intermeddling" under Section 45 of the Succession Act.
